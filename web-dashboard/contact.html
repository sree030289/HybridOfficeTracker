Frequently Asked Questions

Is OfficeTrack really free?

Yes, OfficeTrack is completely free to use. All core features including attendance tracking, analytics, and planning are available at no cost.

How is my privacy protected?

All your data is stored locally on your device only. We never collect, transmit, or store your attendance information on our servers.

Can I use this for official attendance?

OfficeTrack is designed as a personal productivity tool. Please check with your employer about official attendance tracking requirements.

How accurate is location detection?

Location detection uses GPS and typically has an accuracy of 200 meters. You can always override automatic detection with manual entries.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your attendance data as PDF reports directly from the app's analytics section.

What if I switch phones?

Since data is stored locally, you'll need to manually transfer or re-enter your attendance history on a new device.
